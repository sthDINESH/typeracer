How to Use TypeRacer
  1. Select your desired difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the sample text displayed.
  3. Click inside the typing area and begin typing the sample text. The timer will start automatically.
  4. As you type, correct words will be highlighted in blue and mistakes in red.
  5. When you finish typing, press the Enter key to stop the test.
  6. Your time, words per minute (WPM), and results will be displayed on the right.
  7. Click the Retry button to try again with a new sample for the same difficulty.

Tip: Try to type as accurately and quickly as possible for the best score!
